Natural Rights:

What is a Natural Right?

A natural right is a right given by being human that is superior to government and can not be given nor taken away.

Examples of Natural Rights:

An example of a natural right is the right to live. I'm joking, that one's obvious.

Another example is working together, working as a team can't be taken away.

And another example is the ability to create.

And last example is the right to revolution, to be able to overthrow an unfair or oppressive government.

What I believe:

I believe that natural rights are necessary, and do exist because of our devinely given agency from God.

Man wasn't created to rule over, but to learn stuff. But it's still very useful to have a wise leader, because some people are not super smart.
